Accommodation

A Home Away From Home

CAAD provides separate hostel accommodations for boys and girls within the campus, specifically designed for architecture education with spaces that facilitate both collaborative and individual learning.

Amenities

Hostel Facilities

  • Separate accommodations for boys and girls within the campus
  • Rooms with task-lighting that facilitate both collaborative and individual learning
  • Wi-Fi Enabled Hostel for use of technology in learning
  • Nutritious meal options based on student food committee preferences
  • A leisure lifestyle environment promoting cohesive campus learning
  • Round-the-clock security personnel for student safety
