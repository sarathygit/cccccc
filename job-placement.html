Job Placement

Our graduates excel at leading architecture and design firms across India

CAAD graduates are recruited by top architecture firms and design studios across India. Our placement cell ensures students transition seamlessly from academic excellence to professional success, with placements spanning Chennai, Bangalore, Hyderabad, Mumbai, Madurai and beyond.

Batch-wise Results

Placement Track Record

Batch 2017–22

Graduates placed at firms including AULA Designs, Evolve Studio, Cascade Designs, Kosh, Unicorn Designer UI/UX, KR Associates, Archi Wing, and more. Placements across Chennai, Hyderabad, and freelance practice.

Batch 2016–21

Students placed at leading firms across South India including FHD Consultants, Level Play Architects, Vivek and Associates, P Dsyns, Deejos, and UDS Architects in cities like Chennai, Madurai and Bangalore.

Batch 2015–20

A strong cohort with placements at Ashraf Design Studio and other reputed firms. Students pursued careers in architectural design, interior design, urban planning, and freelance architecture practice.

Batch 2014–19

Pioneer batch graduates established themselves in the industry across multiple cities, with several alumni now running their own successful architecture practices and design studios.
