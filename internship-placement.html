Internship Placement

Hands-on professional training at leading architecture studios

Professional Training

Real-World Experience During Your Studies

CAAD ensures every student gains practical experience through structured internship placements at renowned architecture firms and design studios. Students are placed at firms across Chennai, Bangalore, Hyderabad, and other major cities.

Internship periods include dedicated training at firms like Archscape, Keystone Architects, 3Fold Design, Preethi Architects, Ostraca, and many more leading studios across India.

Industry-Integrated Curriculum

Structured internship periods are integrated into the academic calendar, ensuring students gain 6+ months of real-world professional experience before graduation.
