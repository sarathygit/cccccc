Academic Excellence

Elite Academicians Guiding Your Journey

An elite panel of motivated academicians have enrolled with Chennai Academy of Architecture and Design to guide the pupils in their journey through architecture education.

Well researched teaching methodology at par with international standards to equip learners with necessary competencies for leadership roles in construction and design sectors.

Exposure

Holistic Development Beyond Academics

Relevant additional training will be given to students in the areas of soft skills enhancement, management and team work to survive the competition in the market.

At CAAD, we are determined to provide the conducive environment and exposure for all students to evolve as balanced individuals with sound ethics and morals.

Infrastructure

What Sets Us Apart

The Management team at CAAD strongly believes that the Infrastructure equally important as the teaching resources to achieve the set goals for any Institution.

  • Standalone campus designed exclusively for architecture education
  • Creative learning environment fostering innovation
  • Hands-on construction site exposure for practical learning
  • Multi-level air-conditioned library with green terrace extension
  • National and international journal access
  • Equipped workshop facilities for model-making and construction
  • Separate boy and girl hostels on campus
  • Wi-Fi enabled facilities throughout the campus
  • Air-conditioned transportation across Chennai
  • International university MOUs for exchange programs
  • Specialised annual foreign campus training programmes
