New Location

CAAD City Campus

CAAD has recently moved to a bigger, vibrant City Campus at Parivakkam, Poonamallee Bypass, next to the CMRL Poonamallee Metro Depot Station.

Our new campus is designed to inspire creativity with modern studios, well-equipped labs, and spaces that foster collaboration and innovation in design thinking.

Our Facilities

A/C Smart Classrooms

Air-conditioned classrooms with smart boards, projectors, and modern teaching aids for an optimal learning environment.

Design Studios

Spacious design studios with natural lighting, drafting tables, and dedicated space for model-making and creative work.

Workshop Labs

Fully equipped workshops for carpentry, model-making, and material experimentation with professional-grade tools.

Lighting Lab

Dedicated lighting laboratory to study natural and artificial lighting effects on architectural spaces.

Materials Museum

A curated collection of building materials, finishes, and textures for hands-on learning and specification.

A/C Library

Well-stocked air-conditioned library with architecture books, journals, magazines, and digital resources.

Computer Lab

Modern computer lab with AutoCAD, SketchUp, Revit, 3ds Max, and rendering software for digital design.

A/C Auditorium

Air-conditioned auditorium for lectures, seminars, exhibitions, and cultural events.
