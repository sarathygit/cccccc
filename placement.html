Career Success

Shaping Future-Ready Architects & Designers

CAAD's placement cell works tirelessly to connect graduates with leading architecture firms, design studios, and construction companies across India and abroad. Our students are recruited by top firms in Chennai, Bangalore, Hyderabad, Mumbai, and international studios.

From internships during academic years to full-time placements upon graduation, CAAD ensures every student has a clear pathway to professional success.

Industry Connect

Bridging Academia & Professional Practice

CAAD's curriculum integrates real-world exposure through site visits, live projects, and industry workshops. Students are mentored by practicing architects who bring professional insights directly into the classroom.

Our graduates are recruited by firms including AULA Designs, Evolve Studio, Cascade Designs, UDS Architects, KR Associates, FHD Consultants, Level Play Architects, and many more across India.
