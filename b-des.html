B.Des Interior Design

4-Year Bachelor's Degree Program

Program Overview

Master the Art of Interior Design

The Bachelor of Design (B.Des) in Interior Design is a 4-year program that transforms creative minds into professional interior designers. This industry-focused program covers space planning, materials, lighting, and 3D visualization.

Integrated with Alagappa University, our B.Des program focuses on interior design, product design, and creative thinking. No NATA entrance exam is required — students from any stream in 12th can apply.

Our graduates work with leading interior design firms, set up their own studios, or specialize in areas like residential, commercial, hospitality, or exhibition design.

Program Details

  • Duration 4 Years (8 Semesters)
  • Degree Bachelor of Design
  • University Alagappa University
  • Entrance No NATA Required
