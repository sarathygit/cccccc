Laboratories

Workshops

Specialized Labs & Workshops

01

Lighting and Illumination Lab

Study natural and artificial lighting effects on architectural spaces and interior environments through hands-on experiments.

02

Surveying Lab

Land surveying equipment and instruments for comprehensive site analysis, measurement techniques, and topographical studies.

03

Climatology Lab

Climate analysis tools for understanding environmental factors and their influence on sustainable architectural design.

04

Model Making Workshop

Fully equipped workshop for creating architectural scale models, prototypes, and physical design representations.

05

Construction and Carpentry Workshop

Hands-on carpentry and construction workshop for learning building techniques, material properties, and construction methods.
