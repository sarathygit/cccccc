About CAAD

Shaping Architects & Designers Since 1999

The Chennai Academy of Architecture and Design has been at the forefront of design education in South India for over two decades. Our workshop-based experiential learning approach nurtures independent thinkers who create spaces that inspire and endure.

Affiliated to Anna University and approved by the Council of Architecture, CAAD offers internationally recognized programs with faculty from leading design firms.
