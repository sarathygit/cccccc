Courses Offered

Comprehensive programs designed to transform passionate students into industry-ready professionals

01

B.Arch. (5 Years)

Course Title: Bachelor of Architecture

Eligibility: Candidates must have passed 12th or a Diploma qualification. The applicant should have taken Maths as a subject of study in the 12th grade or in the Diploma.

The applicant should have cleared the NATA 2024 or JEE Paper II.

02

B.Des. Interior Design (4 Years)

Course Title: Bachelor of Design

Eligibility: 12th or Diploma or any equivalent study

Affiliation: CAAD is a collaborative institution of Alagappa University whose courses are approved by UGC of India.

Overview: Throughout the program, our students learn various aspects of interior design, including spatial planning, aesthetics, functionality, sustainability, and user-centric design.

03

B.Des. Communication Design (4 Years)

Course Title: Bachelor of Design

Eligibility: 12th or Diploma or any equivalent study

Affiliation: CAAD is a collaborative institution of Alagappa University whose courses are approved by UGC of India.

Overview: A balanced blend of theoretical knowledge and hands-on practical training to help you convey messages effectively through imagery, typography, and layout.

04

B.Des. Industrial Design (4 Years)

Course Title: Bachelor of Design

Eligibility: 12th or Diploma or any equivalent study

Affiliation: CAAD is a collaborative institution of Alagappa University whose courses are approved by UGC of India.

Overview: The course covers principles of industrial design, exploring concepts such as product design, engineering, innovation, and business studies.

05

B.Des. Fashion Design (4 Years)

Course Title: Bachelor of Design

Eligibility: 12th or Diploma or any equivalent study

Affiliation: CAAD is a collaborative institution of Alagappa University whose courses are approved by UGC of India.

Overview: We equip you with the skills, knowledge, and creative vision needed to excel in the dynamic fashion industry, from garment construction to trend forecasting.

06

D.Arch. Interior Design & Decoration (3 Years)

Course Title: Diploma in Architecture

Eligibility: 10th Pass

Affiliation: Approved by Council of Architecture, New Delhi. Affiliated to DOTE – Directorate of Technical Education, Tamil Nadu.

Overview: Hands-on studio courses working on design projects, exploring building materials, construction techniques, and architectural drawing.

07

D.Des. Interior Design (4 Years)

Course Title: Diploma in Design

Eligibility: 12th or Diploma or any equivalent study

Affiliation: CAAD is a collaborative institution of Alagappa University whose courses are approved by UGC of India.

Overview: Understanding design fundamentals, elements of design, and architectural drawing techniques including manual drafting and CAD.

08

M.S. (1 Year)

Course Title: PG in Medical Architecture

Eligibility: Candidates with a valid degree in the following discipline can apply: Architects, Civil Engineers, or Medical Professionals.
