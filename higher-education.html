Higher Education

CAAD graduates at top global universities

Global Pathways

What Makes CAAD Students the Choice of Top Institutions

CAAD graduates are accepted at prestigious universities worldwide including De Montfort University Leicester, Heriot Watt University, SKEMA Business School, and the University of Edinburgh among others.

Our comprehensive curriculum, international studio exposure with SCI-Arc USA, and focus on cutting-edge design technology prepare students for advanced studies at the world's best institutions.

Strengths

Strengths of CAAD Students

Tutored by Experienced Faculty

Our students are mentored by experienced faculty members from the industry who bring real-world architectural knowledge and academic rigour, preparing them for the demands of postgraduate programs at global institutions.

Undergone International Studios

Through MOU partnerships with Southern California Institute of Architecture (SCI-Arc), students gain international studio experience, making them familiar with global pedagogical standards and design methodologies.

Proficient with Latest Tools in AI

CAAD students are trained in the latest tools in Artificial Intelligence, computational design, and digital fabrication, giving them a competitive edge in applications to advanced programs worldwide.
