B.Arch Architecture

5-Year Professional Degree Program

Program Overview

Become a Professional Architect

The Bachelor of Architecture (B.Arch) is a 5-year professional degree program that prepares students for a career in architecture. This comprehensive program covers architectural design, building technology, history of architecture, and professional practice.

Approved by the Council of Architecture (COA) and affiliated to Anna University, Chennai, our B.Arch program produces graduates who are ready to design buildings, plan spaces, and shape the built environment.

Architecture is a 100% self-employable profession. Our graduates go on to start their own practices, work with leading firms, or pursue higher studies globally.
