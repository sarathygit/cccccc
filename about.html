About CAAD

Shaping architects and designers since 1999

Our Story

A Legacy of Excellence in Design Education

Founded under the guidance of Shri Jayendra Saraswathi Swamigal of Kanchi Kamakoti Peetham, the Chennai Academy of Architecture and Design has been at the forefront of design education in South India for over two decades.

CAAD is a standalone campus dedicated exclusively to Architecture and Design, offering workshop-based experiential learning that nurtures independent thinkers and design problem-solvers.

Our programs are designed to foster creativity, technical mastery, and a deep understanding of human needs—creating graduates who go on to shape the built environment.

Our Vision

To be a center of excellence in architecture and design education, producing globally competent professionals who contribute meaningfully to the built environment.

Our Mission

To provide workshop-based experiential learning, foster design-solving skills, and nurture creative thinkers who can envision and create spaces that inspire and endure.

Our Values

Academic excellence, creative freedom, industry relevance, ethical practice, and holistic development of every student who walks through our doors.
